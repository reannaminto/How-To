How To Make Chocolate Chip Cookies

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375℉.










Step 2: Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl











Step 3: Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy.










Step 4: Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.











Step 5: Gradually beat in flour mixture.










Step 6: Stir in morsels (and nuts if desired).










Step 7: Drop dough by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.











Step 8: Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown.











Step 9: Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes then remove to wire racks to cool completely.











Step 10: Enjoy your fresh, warm, and chewy chocolate chip cookies!
